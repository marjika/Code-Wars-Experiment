Snakes and Ladders
Click the dice and roll to advance farther
on the board. If you land on a ladder,
you will climb up the board, but landing
on a snake's head will slide you back down
the board. Roll doubles and get another turn.
Player one is a black token, and player two
is purple. If you choose the "One Player"
option, the computer is player 2. Reach the
100th square to win.
